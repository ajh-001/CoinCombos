How to fill the table manually

  1. Line 1: Use the maximum possible number of the largest coins available in your box, then the next largest, until you hit the target. (Greedy approach).
  2. Next Lines: Look at your last row from right to left.
  3. Find the first column (that isn't 1p) with a count greater than 0.
  4. Decrease that count by 1. Keep everything to its left the exact same.
  5. Fill the Remainder: Fill everything to its right using the Greedy approach from step 1.
    What if I run out of coins? If you exhaust your supply of smaller coins before reaching the total, that row is a "Dead End." Cross it out (do not record it) and return to Step 4 to find the next possible number to decrease.
  6. Done: You are finished when you can no longer apply step 4, or only the 1p column has a value.
